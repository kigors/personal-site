Emotion detection project

#python #tensorflow #cnn #tranfer-learning #BiT #inception #valence-arousal

Description:

I analyzed different training strategies of convolutional neural networks (CNN) for emotion detection based on images (face photos). I experimented with Inception V3 and BiT-M r50x1 pre-trained networks (trained on ImageNet), compared fine-tuning and feature extraction means of transfer learning.

I trained models on images provided by Kaggle competition experimenting with original images and images of faces cropped by openCV face detector.

Final experiment is dedicated to valence-arousal representation of emotions. Using same dataset I trained models (regressor and regressor+classifier) to predict valence and arousal of emotion on the image.

More information and demo script for web camera are available on GitHub repository.

Megafon project

#python #statistics #hypothesis_testing

Description:

Like any business, Megafon wants to increase the satisfaction of its customers with the quality of services. This is an important task for retaining users - both long-term and acquired ones. After all, marketing and promotion costs will not be justified if the client leaves due to poor communication quality. However, due to the limited resources, technical department can solve a finite number of problems.

To do this effectively, it is important to determine which technical indicators of communication quality are the strongest for customer satisfaction and focus resources to improve them. Therefore, Megafon conducted a survey of its customers, asking them to assess the level of satisfaction with the quality of communication. Technical metrics were collected for each customer who completed the survey.

The task is to prepare a study for Megafon and analyze how the score that the client puts in the survey depends (if it depends) on the collected technical indicators.

Study summary:

Data was analysed and a series of hypothesis was stated to be proved or rejected in the study. Bootstrapping was the primary method for testing hypothesises.

More information is available in Colab notebook.

Chatbot on RNN

#python #tensorflow #rnn #attention #chatbot

Description:

In attached notebook I experimented with several RNN (recurrent neural net) designs to create a simple chat-bot trained on Cornell Movie-Dialogs Corpus.
3 different approached where tested:
  1. char-RNN with encoder/decoder: predicting a phrase symbol-by-symbol
  2. encoder/decoder RNN on tokens: predicting a phrase by words
  3. encoder/decoder RNN with attention: implementation of attention mechanism for chat bot

I studied dependence of model parameters selection (number of LSTM layers, units, embedding size, sequence length, etc.) on predicting subjective quality trying to maximize model complexity running on a signle GPU.

Study is available on Colab.

The following github repository is dedicated to deployment of the third model with tensorflow-model-serving:
https://github.com/kigors/tf-serving-chatbot-attention

Description:

Model is based on trained VGGFace model using transfer learning technique by adding and retraining 2 extra fully connected layers with 3 outputs for:
  • gender prediction (binary classifier)
  • race prediction (categorical classifier)
  • age prediction (regression)

Курсовой проект №3

Описание работы:

Любой бизнес хочет максимизировать количество клиентов. Для достижения этой цели важно не только пытаться привлечь новых, но и удерживать уже существующих. Удержать клиента обойдется компании дешевле, чем привлечь нового. Кроме того, новый клиент может оказаться слабо заинтересованным в услугах бизнеса и с ним будет сложно работать, тогда как о старых клиентах уже есть необходимые данные по взаимодействию с сервисом.

Соответственно, прогнозируя отток, мы можем вовремя среагировать и попытаться удержать клиента, который хочет уйти. Опираясь на данные об услугах, которыми пользуется клиент, мы можем сделать ему специальное предложение, пытаясь изменить его решение об уходе от оператора. Благодаря этому задача удержания будет легче в реализации, чем задача привлечения новых пользователей, о которых мы еще ничего не знаем.

Вам предоставлен набор данных от телекоммуникационной компании. В данных содержится информация о почти шести тысячах пользователей, их демографических характеристиках, услугах, которыми они пользуются, длительности пользования услугами оператора, методе оплаты, размере оплаты.

Cтоит задача проанализировать данные и спрогнозировать отток пользователей (выявить людей, которые продлят контракт и которые не продлят).
